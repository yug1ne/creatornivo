## Campaign Operations Checklist

- Confirm the legitimate campaign purpose.
- Confirm ICP and segmentation.
- Verify every prospect’s company and current role.
- Verify every email address.
- Document the contact-data source.
- Review source usage terms.
- Remove suppressed contacts.
- Remove existing customers when required.
- Remove active opportunities.
- Confirm applicable jurisdictions.
- Complete legal and privacy review.
- Verify sender identity.
- Verify sending-domain ownership.
- Review current mailbox-provider requirements.
- Verify required domain authentication.
- Test From, Reply-To, and signature details.
- Verify all claims and customer evidence.
- Verify every personalization source.
- Test missing-variable fallbacks.
- Remove sensitive personalization.
- Verify referral permission.
- Test all links.
- Test calendar availability and meeting duration.
- Confirm opt-out processing.
- Test global and campaign suppression.
- Confirm sequence stop conditions.
- Confirm human reply ownership.
- Confirm response-time expectations.
- Test positive, negative, referral, and opt-out replies.
- Confirm bounce and complaint handling.
- Review volume and sending cadence.
- Test plain-text and mobile rendering.
- Review tracking necessity and disclosure.
- Confirm attachment security.
- Review regulated-industry restrictions.
- Send an internal seed test.
- Manually review high-value accounts.
- Monitor early campaign quality before scaling.
- Pause when complaints, bounces, or negative feedback indicate poor quality.
